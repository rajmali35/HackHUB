About the Event

Welcome to PVG'S HACK HUB, a 24-hour national-level coding marathon where innovation meets creativity! This hackathon aims to bring together the brightest minds to solve real-world challenges using technology. Whether you are a beginner or a pro, this is your chance to showcase your skills, build amazing solutions, and connect with like-minded innovators.

Hackathon Themes

🤖 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Build intelligent solutions using AI/ML technologies to solve complex problems and automate processes.

💻 Web & App Development

Create innovative web applications and mobile apps that enhance user experience and solve everyday challenges.

🏥 Healthcare & Education

Develop solutions that improve healthcare delivery and educational experiences for better outcomes.

🏙️ Smart Cities & Agriculture

Build sustainable solutions for urban development and agricultural innovation using IoT and smart technologies.

💡 Open Innovation (Wildcard)

Think outside the box! Present your most creative and innovative ideas that don't fit traditional categories.
